ABOUT ME & DAL CRAFTS

When I started my artistic journey they said "mix media", so I did, and I took it as a challenge to do so.
From drawing to 3D modeling to photography, I am always experimenting with new techniques. Mixing and matching media to create truly one-of-a-kind pieces, all while honing my skills for the individual crafts.
Every project poses a unique challenge, utilizing my diversity of skills and experiance to produce a peice personalized art.
So I started DAL Crafts to document my journey along the way. I am excited to share my passion with the world and connect with other creators who share my love of all things artistic.
